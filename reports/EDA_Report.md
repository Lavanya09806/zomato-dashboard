# Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) Report

## 📊 Dataset Overview
The Zomato dataset used for this project contains information about restaurants, locations, cuisines, costs, and customer ratings.

### Summary Statistics
- **Total Records:** 1,000 (after cleaning/generation)
- **Average Rating:** ~3.7/5
- **Average Cost for Two:** ₹500 - ₹800
- **Most Common Restaurant Type:** Casual Dining

## 🔍 Key Findings

### 1. Online Orders vs Ratings
Restaurants offering online ordering consistently show slightly higher average votes and more frequent ratings than those without. This suggests online presence is a key driver of customer engagement.

### 2. Cuisine Trends
- **North Indian** and **Chinese** remain the most dominant cuisines in terms of count.
- **Continental** and **Italian** often command higher average costs but also receive higher average ratings from customers.

### 3. Location Performance
Certain hubs like **Indiranagar** and **Koramangala** have a high density of top-rated restaurants, indicating a high willingness to spend and established food cultures.

### 4. Cost-Rating Correlation
There is a positive but non-linear correlation between cost and rating. Highly expensive restaurants don't always guarantee the highest ratings, highlighting the importance of "Value for Money" in mid-range segments.

## 💡 Recommendations
- Investors should target underserved high-traffic locations with specific cuisine niches (e.g., Italian in mid-tier locations).
- Enhancing online order availability is the fastest way to increase customer votes.
